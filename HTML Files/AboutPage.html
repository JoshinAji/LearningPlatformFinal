    • About Us

    Xavier Care Home is a Care Home for children which comes under the The Priests of the Sacred Heart of Jesus of Bétharram Congregation. The Priests of the Sacred Heart of Jesus of Betharram are a group of male Roman Catholic clergy who belong to the Pontifical Right. As the realization of a dream, St. Michael Garicoits founded it at Bétharram in 1832. This community, which was devoted to the Sacred Heart, was charged with evangelizing the populace through missions and instructing the young. After Garicoits' passing, the Pope officially approved it.

    Social services have made some progress, but many kids still lack a stable home. By offering a secure and nurturing environment, orphanages offer a short-term solution to the issue. Living at an orphanage can be a good experience for some kids because they can develop relationships with the staff and other kids and get the help they need to grow.
